Latitude vs. Temperature

The purpuse of this Analysis was to find out wether global latitude has an effect on Temperature. In other words to figure out if there's a correlation of geographical location and how hot/cold the city is

As we can see by the distribution on the scatter plot above, there is a correlation of temperature and geographical locaiton. The closer the city is to the equator, the hotter it will be.
